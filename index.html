Our Vision

Founder's Story

The founder has struggled with ADHD his whole life. He has many friends who are also neurodiverse. This community often struggles with tasks like paying bills, finding jobs, and applying for work online because these tasks are often designed for neurotypical mindsets. For example, he has a friend who loves trains and dreams of working for a train company, but because of his dyslexia, he has never felt able to craft an effective CV. Instead, he works at Morrisons and watches the trains at lunchtime. The founder believes that the millions of neurodiverse people in the UK deserve an opportunity to find their dream jobs. This includes people with dyslexia, ADHD, and other impediments that stop them from reaching their potential in society. This is an unprecedented economic opportunity to help an underappreciated community.

Concept:

A mobile app that supports neurodiverse people to get their dream jobs would achieve this vision. As a platform, it makes it easy for these users to demonstrate their capability and match the users with employers looking for creative and diverse talent. The features of this app include: easy-to-make video selfies with tutorials on how to present yourself to prospective employers; personal development plan support, and access to support such as chat, phone numbers, and relevant charities. These features enable users to showcase their best selves without constraining them with traditional wordy job application tools. This is a non-profit project to create positive change, offering an inclusive and supportive platform that addresses the unique needs of neurodiverse people.
